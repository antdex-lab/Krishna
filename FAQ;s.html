FAQS

01. What types of fabrics do you offer?

We offer a wide range of fabrics, including Viscose, cotton, polyester, nylon, and many more.

02. Can I order custom fabrics?

Yes, we offer custom fabric solutions tailored to your specific needs and requirements. Please contact us to discuss your project and we will work with you to find the perfect solution.

03. What industries do you supply fabrics to?

We supply fabrics to a wide range of industries, including fashion and home decor.

04. What is your quality control process?

We have a strict quality control process in place to ensure that every fabric we produce meets the highest standards of quality and performance. Our team of experts inspects each fabric at every stage of the production process to ensure that it meets our rigorous standards.

05. How do I place an order?

You can place an order by phone or by email. Our team of experts will be happy to assist you with your order and answer any questions you may have.

06. What is your shipping policy?

We offer shipping to customers worldwide. Shipping rates and times may vary depending on your location and the size of your order. Please contact us for more information on our shipping policies.

07. What is your return policy?

Please contact us for more information on our return policies.

08. Do you offer samples?

Yes, we offer fabric samples upon request. Please contact us to request samples and we will be happy to assist you.
