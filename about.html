01. Our Mission

Our goal is to offer our customers the highest-quality materials that satisfy their unique needs and specifications. By providing great customer service and creative solutions, we work hard to go above and beyond what our customers expect. We are dedicated to adopting sustainable production techniques, and we think it is important to give back to the communities we work with.

02. Our History

Krishna Textiles is a subsidiary of Het Gems, which is a diamond manufacturing unit located in Surat, India. Surat is a hub for diamond cutting and polishing, and Het Gems is one of the many companies in the city that specializes in producing high-quality diamonds.The history of Somtex Fab business in the textile sector is extensive. We were established in 2020 and have been producing premium fabrics for more than 3 years. We have stayed dedicated to creating materials that are not only aesthetically pleasing but also long-lasting and useful throughout our history.Our founders had a dream of making fabrics that would endure the test of time since they were passionate about the textile business. They started by making investments in modern equipment and engaging a group of talented craftsmen to create the best fabrics in the business. Our business developed and flourished through time, earning a reputation for excellence and innovation.We have consistently pushed the limits of what is feasible in the production of fabric throughout the years, maintaining our leadership in the sector. We have created fresh materials and technological advancements and integrated sustainable procedures into our manufacturing process.We are happy to serve clients in a variety of industries worldwide as a leading manufacturer of premium fabrics today. We remain strongly committed to quality and innovation, and we anticipate providing our customers with excellent service for many years to come.

03. Our Vision

Our vision is to become the leader in the worldwide textile market and establish a reputation for quality, innovation, and sustainability. We will accomplish this by making ongoing investments in our people, our infrastructure, and our operational procedures, as well as by continuing to be on the very forefront of business advancements. We will keep hearing from our clients, do our best to anticipate their needs, and constantly aim to go above and beyond their expectations.
