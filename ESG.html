Environment, health & safety

The Company believes that a robust environment, health and safety (EHS) framework is critical for long-term growth.

Health & Safety
at Krishna Textiles.

At Krishna Textiles, the well-being of our workforce is our top priority. We ensure a safe and healthy work environment through various initiatives:
- Providing protective safety gear such as earplugs, gloves, helmets, and masks to all plant workers.
- Offering nutritious meals at a subsidized cost in our company canteen.
- Insuring our workforce under Workmen Compensation and Group Medical Policy, along with conducting regular health check-ups for employees and workers.
- During COVID-19, we supported our employees by funding vaccinations, enabling work-from-home options, implementing flexible working hours, and providing conveyance facilities.

Environment Commitment

Sustainable Energy at Krishna Textiles

At Krishna Textiles, we are committed to sustainable manufacturing. We generate over 30% of our energy requirements through our windmills and solar power plants, reducing our carbon footprint and promoting eco-friendly production. Our dedication to renewable energy ensures a greener future while maintaining the highest quality in textile manufacturing.

Water Conservation at Krishna Textiles

At Krishna Textiles, we are committed to sustainable water management. Our dyeing & processing facility is equipped with a state-of-the-art Effluent Treatment Plant, allowing us to efficiently recycle and reuse water consumed in our dyeing processes. This initiative helps us minimize waste, conserve natural resources, and reduce our environmental impact while maintaining the highest quality standards in textile production.

Green Initiatives at Krishna Textiles

At Krishna Textiles, we believe in harmonizing industry with nature. We have dedicated over 20,000 sq. ft. across our manufacturing facilities for plantation and greenery, creating a healthier and more sustainable environment. Our commitment to green spaces not only enhances biodiversity but also contributes to a cleaner and greener future.

Clean Air Commitment at Krishna Textiles

At Krishna Textiles, we prioritize sustainable manufacturing practices to reduce our environmental footprint. Instead of coal, we use eco-friendly briquettes made from agricultural waste in our boilers for steam generation. This cleaner energy alternative helps reduce carbon emissions and supports our commitment to a greener and more sustainable future.
